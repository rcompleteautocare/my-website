# Summer Uniform & Appearance SOP

**Document owner:** Management
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective:** 2026-07-15 (effective immediately)
**Applies to:** All employees

## Mission

Create a clean, professional, trustworthy appearance that reflects the quality
of R Complete Auto Care. Every employee represents the company from the moment
they arrive until they leave.

## Summer uniform policy

**Required:**

- Official R Complete Auto Care company T-shirt
- Clean black work pants **or** approved black work shorts (management approval
  required)
- Black work boots or approved safety shoes
- Belt (if applicable)
- Safety glasses available while working
- Company hat (optional, if approved)

**Not allowed:**

- Ripped clothing
- Offensive logos
- Non-company shirts while working
- Sandals or open-toe shoes

## Shirt policy

Every employee will:

- Arrive in a clean company shirt.
- Change shirts if they get excessively dirty during the workday.
- Keep one spare clean company shirt at the shop.
- Replace damaged or heavily stained shirts immediately.

Management maintains spare shirts for emergencies and new hires.

## Personal appearance

Employees are expected to maintain:

- Good personal hygiene
- Clean hands when greeting customers
- Groomed hair
- Trimmed facial hair
- Clean fingernails
- Professional appearance

Smoking or vaping should never occur in front of customers.

## Customer greeting standard

Within **5 seconds** of a customer entering:

1. Smile
2. Make eye contact
3. Stand if seated
4. Welcome the customer

**Script:**

> "Good morning! Welcome to R Complete Auto Care. How can we help you today?"

**Never ignore a customer.**

## Customer communication

**Always:**

- Speak professionally
- Be respectful
- Listen without interrupting
- Thank customers
- Explain repairs clearly

**Never:**

- Argue
- Use profanity
- Speak negatively about previous repair shops
- Blame customers
- Guess at repairs

**If you don't know an answer:**

> "That's a great question. Let me verify that information so I can give you the
> correct answer."

## Shop cleanliness

The shop should always be customer-ready.

- [ ] Clean waiting room
- [ ] Vacuum daily
- [ ] Empty trash
- [ ] Clean counters
- [ ] Clean restroom
- [ ] Organized front desk
- [ ] Coffee/snack station stocked
- [ ] Parking lot free of debris

## Vehicle cleanliness

**Use:**

- Seat covers
- Floor mats
- Steering wheel covers
- Fender covers when appropriate

**Return every vehicle:**

- Clean
- No fingerprints
- No grease
- No tools left inside

## Professional expectations

Employees should conduct themselves as professionals at all times.

Customers should feel:

- Confident
- Comfortable
- Respected
- Informed

Every interaction should reinforce that R Complete Auto Care is the most trusted
repair shop in Northwest Indiana.
